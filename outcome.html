OUTCOME

The analytics data showed a 34 percent organic increase in international traffic through search engines with an overall 4% bounce rate reduction. This was achieved over a period of five months and was compared to the corresponding period of the same five months the previous year before the revamp of the site. Also, four out of five target keywords showed up on page 1 on Google search where they previously showed up from pages 3 - 10. Top management was very pleased with both the achievements and the significantly improved aesthetics, and our unit received a Staff Achievement Award from the President.
